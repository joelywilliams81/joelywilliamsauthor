About Me

Joely Williams is a writer, poet, and community advocate originally from the Bronx, NY and now based in Columbia, SC. Her writing has been published in magazines, newspapers, and the poetry anthology Liminal Women: A Drink From The Wellspring. She blends themes of identity, healing, and place into her work, always centering the power of voice and story. Joely is currently working on her second book and a poetry collection.
