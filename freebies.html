How to use these

Layer the downloads into your rituals: read a poem aloud to open your journaling hour, incorporate the FLOW prompts into classroom circles, or adapt the workshop plan to co-create with your community. Highlight what resonates, add your own notes in the margins, and let the pages travel with you from space to space.
