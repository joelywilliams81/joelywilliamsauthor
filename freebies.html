How to use these

Set the mood with music, light, or breathwork, then journey through a few pages together. Invite participants to journal between sections, voice what resonates, and note any prompts to revisit later. Layer the downloads into your rituals: read a poem aloud to open your journaling hour, incorporate the FLOW prompts into classroom circles, or adapt the workshop plan to co-create with your community. Highlight what resonates, add your own notes in the margins, and let the pages travel with you from space to space.
