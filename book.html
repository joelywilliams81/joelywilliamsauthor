Put the Phone Down, We Have a Job to Do is Joely Williams' debut trauma healing workbook. This book invites readers into reflection through writing, art, literature, psychology, and personal storytelling.

Designed to be practical and powerful, this is the first in a series of works that explore deep healing and honest reconnection with the self and community.
