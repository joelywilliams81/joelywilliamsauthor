Put the Phone Down, We Have a Job to Do

Put the Phone Down, We Have a Job to Do is a trauma healing workbook that offers readers a powerful space for reflection, artistic expression, and personal restoration. Combining writing prompts, tasks, and insights rooted in psychology, literature, and storytelling, this book is a call to action for anyone ready to face their trauma with intention and creative courage.
