Custom Poetry for Life's Sacred Moments

Poetry can bless a room, hold a memory, and gather people into a shared breath. Let's create words that honor your milestone and the people you love.

What I offer

From intimate gatherings to large-scale programs, I craft original poems, speeches, and scripts that reflect the intention behind your event. Each piece is rooted in attentive listening and collaborative storytelling.

Occasions include

  • Weddings, vow renewals, and anniversary celebrations
  • Funerals, celebrations of life, and memorial tributes
  • Birthdays, baby showers, and coming-of-age moments
  • Graduations, retirements, and professional milestones
  • Community, nonprofit, and corporate gatherings

How it works

Discovery call

We begin with a conversation about your event, the people we're honoring, and the emotional tone you want to set. This can happen by phone, video, or a thoughtful questionnaire.

Collaborative drafting

After gathering your stories, I draft a piece that reflects your voice and values. We review together, invite edits, and ensure the language feels aligned with your intentions.

Delivery & support

You'll receive a polished written piece, along with optional performance notes and a recorded read-through so you can feel confident bringing the words into the room.

Investment

Custom commissions begin at $350 and vary based on length, turnaround time, and performance requests. Sliding scale pricing is available for community-centered events and mutual aid projects.

What you receive

  • Original poem or speech tailored to your event
  • Two rounds of collaborative revisions
  • Optional audio recording and performance coaching tips
  • Print-ready and digital copies for sharing with loved ones
